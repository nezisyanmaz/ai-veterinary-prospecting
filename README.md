# ai-veterinary-prospecting
# AI Veterinary Prospecting Workflow

## Overview

This project explores how AI can help automate and improve outbound prospecting for B2B healthcare software companies.

Instead of manually researching every account, the goal is to combine AI and sales tools to identify high-potential veterinary practices, generate account insights, and prepare personalised outreach while keeping a human in the decision-making loop.

---

## Objective

Build an AI-assisted prospecting workflow that:

- Finds veterinary practices from multiple public sources
- Identifies growth and operational signals
- Qualifies and prioritises accounts
- Finds relevant decision-makers
- Generates CRM-ready account summaries
- Creates personalised outreach drafts

---

## Workflow

Public Data Sources
↓
AI Source Discovery
↓
Apify Data Collection
↓
AI Qualification & Lead Scoring
↓
Apollo Contact Enrichment
↓
CRM
↓
AI Personalised Outreach Draft
↓
Human Review
↓
Send

---

## Tools

- Claude – Research, orchestration and lead qualification
- Apify – Public data collection
- Apollo – Contact enrichment and email verification
- HubSpot – CRM
- ChatGPT / Claude – Personalised outreach generation

---

## Current Status

This repository documents the workflow design, prompts and CRM structure.

The long-term goal is to build a working prototype that automates account research and generates personalised outreach while keeping human approval before sending.
