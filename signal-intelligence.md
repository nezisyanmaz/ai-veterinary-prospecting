## Public Signal Monitoring

The workflow can also monitor public sources beyond standard company databases to identify stronger sales signals before outreach.

Potential sources include:

- Veterinary industry magazines
- Chamber of Commerce announcements
- Conference exhibitor and speaker lists
- Veterinary association directories
- Job boards
- Company news pages
- Google reviews
- Competitor customer and case study pages

AI can collect and classify these signals into categories such as:

- New location
- Active hiring
- New service launch
- Technology investment
- Conference participation
- Industry recognition
- Booking or communication complaints
- Likely incumbent software

These signals are then added to the account record and used to create a more relevant outreach angle.

### Example

**Growth signals**
- Exhibiting at London Vet Show
- Hiring three veterinary nurses
- Opened a second location

**Pain hypothesis**
- Increased administrative workload
- Multi-site coordination
- Greater pressure on booking and communication workflows

The aim is to turn public information into account intelligence before the first sales conversation.
