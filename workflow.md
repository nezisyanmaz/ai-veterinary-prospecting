# AI Prospecting Workflow

## Step 1 - Source Discovery
Claude identifies relevant public sources such as:
- Google Maps
- Industry magazines
- Veterinary directories
- Job boards
- Chamber of Commerce
- Company websites
- Competitor customer pages

## Step 2 - Data Collection
Apify collects:
- Company name
- Website
- Phone number
- Location
- Reviews
- Public company information

## Step 3 - AI Research

Claude researches each account for:

- Growth signals
- Hiring activity
- New services
- Technology adoption
- Competitor software
- Public reviews
- Operational complexity

## Step 4 - Lead Qualification

Each account receives:

- Fit score
- Priority
- Growth signals
- Pain hypotheses
- Suggested outreach angle

## Step 5 - Contact Enrichment

Apollo identifies:

- Decision makers
- Email addresses
- LinkedIn profiles

## Step 6 - CRM

Each account is uploaded with:

- Company profile
- AI notes
- Growth signals
- Pain hypotheses
- Lead score
- Outreach status

## Step 7 - AI Personalisation

AI generates:

- Personalised email drafts
- Discovery questions
- Call preparation notes

Human approval is required before sending.
